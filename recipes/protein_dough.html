Protein_dough

Sometimes you want an easy snack and want to make it in an easy way. Well here you go! The best cookie dough for you!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup protein powder
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup water (for desired texture)
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by whisking together your peanut butter and maple syrup until smooth
  2. Slowly add the protein powder until incorporated
  3. Using your hands, form into a smooth dough
  4. If you prefer a thinner cookie dough, add some water or milk
  5. Fold through the chocolate chips, before transferring to a bowl and enjoying immediately
